Duke de Richelieu

The first monument erected in Odessa - a monument to the Frenchman, Armand Emmanuel du Plessis, Duc de Richelieu.

National Opera and Ballet Theater

The Odessa National Academic Opera and Ballet Theatre is the oldest theatre in Odesa, Ukraine.

Deribasovskaya Street

Vulytsia Derybasivska or De Ribas Street is a pedestrian walkway (street) in the heart of Odesa, Ukraine.

Odessa Passage

Odesa Passage is a shopping arcade and a hotel on Derybasivska Street in the centre of Odesa.

Primorsky Boulevard

Primorsky Boulevard is one of the most prominent streets in Odessa, starting at Dumskaya Square and ending at Vorontsov Palace.

City-Garden

The Odesa City garden is located in town centre of Odesa, Ukraine, at the Derybasivska Street.

Potemkin Stairs

The Potemkin Stairs or Potemkin Steps are a giant stairway in Odesa, Ukraine.

Shevchenko Park

The park was officially opened on 7 September 1875 and on 10 September was named the "Alexander Park" after Alexander II of Russia himself had visited Odesa and planted an oak tree during the park's opening ceremony.

Catacombs of Odessa

The Odesa Catacombs are a labyrinth-like network of tunnels (subterranean cavities) located under the city of Odesa and its outskirts
